# Why Keeping Your Home Clean Between Professional Cleanings Matters.

A Real Example From Our Experience

Hiring a professional cleaning service is a great way to maintain a healthy, comfortable home. However, something many clients don’t realize is that the condition of the home before the cleaning appointment can significantly affect both the final result and the total cost of the service.

Professional cleaners usually work with estimated time slots based on the typical condition of a home. These estimates assume that the space has been maintained at a basic level between cleanings. When a home has excessive buildup of dirt, grease, or clutter, the cleaning process becomes much more complex and time-consuming than expected.

Recently, I had an experience that clearly illustrates this.

My assistant and I were scheduled to clean a small apartment of about 33 square meters. Normally, a home of this size can be cleaned efficiently within a reasonable timeframe. However, due to the level of accumulated dirt and heavy buildup, the cleaning ended up taking five hours, and even after that amount of time the result still wasn’t 100% complete. In reality, the apartment will likely need another full deep cleaning to reach the standard we always aim to deliver.

Situations like this can be challenging for both sides. As cleaning professionals, we take pride in our work and always want to deliver the best possible result. Spending many hours working hard and still feeling that the space has not reached its full potential can be frustrating. At the same time, it can be uncomfortable to inform the client that an additional fee may be necessary due to the extra time and effort required.

This is why maintaining your home in a reasonable state of cleanliness between professional visits is so important.

## Why Basics Maintenance Makes a Big Difference

When a home is kept at a basic level of order and hygiene:

	•	Cleaners can focus on detailed work rather than heavy buildup removal

	•	The results are significantly better

	•	The service can be completed within the expected timeframe

	•	Clients avoid unexpected additional costs

### We Also Love the Challenge

That said, we also genuinely enjoy the challenge of transforming spaces that need extra care. Giving a new life and a fresh start to homes that have been neglected can be extremely rewarding. With the right time, preparation, and expectations, even heavily used spaces can be restored and made comfortable again.

Of course, deep cleaning services are available when a home requires a full reset. However, these services are typically priced and scheduled differently because they require much more time, equipment, and effort.

Our goal is always to deliver excellent results and leave every home feeling fresh and comfortable. By keeping your home in a manageable condition between cleanings, you help ensure the service runs smoothly, efficiently, and without unexpected charges.
